About the EP

Virtual Angel is an experimental album built around the imaginary angels and virtual prophets of the digital age. It reimagines human existence in a digitized world, where “angelic” signals arrive not from the sky but from flows of data. Sounds are broken apart, echoed, and rebuilt like fragments of code.

Tracks:
1. Virtual Angel
2. Neon Prophecy
3. Crystal Eyes
4. Falling Through Signals
